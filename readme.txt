******* Hictu Plugin for WordPress *******

Here's all you need to do:

1) Download and unzip the zip file.

2) You'll have a directory/folder called hictu-widget, put this into 
your plugin directory.

3) Go to your admin panel and activate the plugin.

4) Now go to Options -> hictu

5) Enter the ID of your Hictu widget. (You can take your Hictu Widget ID going to http://www.hictu.com -> Widget -> Generate)

6) Your blog is now powered by audio/video comments!
